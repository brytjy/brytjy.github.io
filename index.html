About Me

Bryan is a penultimate year (graduating September 2020) student at Singapore Institute of Technology reading Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Telematics on a Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) full study sponsorship. He is also an in-service personnel of SAF holding the rank of a ME4A which is equivalent to a second lieutenant.

His interest in technology began at around 13 years old when he was introduced to computer in Secondary School. Now, he is proficient in programming and multimedia production. His track record in programming includes an automated call centre prototype exhibited at the 26th Intelligent Transportation Systems World Congress under NCS's booth, and developing a knowledge management system and mobile application for his SAF Battalion.

Current interest:
- Programming
- Machine learning
- Natural language processing
- Data science
- Multimedia production

Fun facts:
- He loves to travel and occasionally uploads a video compilation on his YouTube channel
- He used to be an aspiring pilot at Singapore Youth Flying Club
